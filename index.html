About the fundraiser:

The Barkery is our business model that utilizes local bakers who specialize in creating enjoyable edibles for your canine and feline friends. In this model bakers make healthy natural treats, with and without cannabinoid byproducts. The bakers use local cannabis farmers CBD oil to create the treats. The healthy and delicious treats are then used by non-profit anumal rescue shelters to treat animals suffering from anxiety, cancers, rashes, old age, and other ailments. The treats can be sold by the non-profits as a fundraiser. The money earned from sales will pay eberyone involved to help take care of these animals.
